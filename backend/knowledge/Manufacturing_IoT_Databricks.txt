Title: Predictive Maintenance using IoT & Databricks
Industry: Manufacturing
Technologies: Azure IoT Hub, Databricks, MLflow, Delta Live Tables
Deployment Year: 2023

Pain Points:
- Unplanned downtime costs $50 k/hour
- Sensor data siloed across 14 plants
- Manual feature engineering took 6 weeks

Solution:
- Streaming ingestion of 3 TB/day sensor data
- Auto-ML in Databricks predicts failures 48 hrs in advance
- MLflow model registry enables CI/CD across plants
- Downtime reduced by 22 %
